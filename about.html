About Frontpoint

Delivering Excellence in Global Sourcing & Supply

Who We Are

Frontpoint Global Enterprise is a trusted provider of strategic procurement and business services, delivering tailored solutions across global trade, supply chain management, RMB payment support, and innovative contracting.

We connect Nigerian buyers with verified Chinese suppliers through transparent processes, strict compliance, and efficient logistics coordination. Our goal is to simplify international procurement while ensuring reliability, quality, and efficiency for our clients worldwide.

Founded in 2025 by Idris Kabo — a businessman, academic researcher, and economist — Frontpoint is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Nigeria under Registration No: RC 9357566.

Sustainability Commitment

At Frontpoint, we believe in building a cleaner and more sustainable future. We encourage the adoption of renewable energy solutions, environmentally responsible products, and low-emission technologies.

By promoting solar energy systems, electric vehicles, and sustainable procurement practices, we help organizations reduce their carbon footprint while supporting the global goal of carbon neutrality by 2050.
